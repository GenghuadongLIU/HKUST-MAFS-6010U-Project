
                        Overview
                        
                            Detecting and preventing criminal activities is now business critical. From money laundering to fraud, cyber-crime and terrorism, machine learning and AI tools and applications are at the core of efforts keeping companies online and operating. At the same time, new privacy enhancing technologies including homomorphic encryption and two-factor authentication are opening up new possibilities for safety.
