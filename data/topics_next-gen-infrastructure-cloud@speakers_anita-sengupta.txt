                About Anita
                
                Dr. Anita Sengupta is an aerospace engineer, veteran of NASA, aviation entrepreneur, and instrument rated pilot. She is a Research Professor of Astronautics at the University of Southern California developing aerospace technologies and co-founder of ASX an eVTOL startup.
